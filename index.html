A few words about us

Vision

Our vision is to provide relevant and interesting educational and extra-mural activities within a caring , safe and stimulating environment. All role players maintaining high moral and proffessional standards so that the children can reach their full potential.

Mission

To prepare students for life by providing them with a well rounded foundational education. At Nozuko Educare Centre we aim to make education fun and interesting in order to foster a love for learning.

Our Programs

Infant Care


Specialized care for infants, including feeding, diaper changing, and sensory stimulation

Pre-Kindergaten classes


Preparing children for the transition to formal schooling by introducing basic concepts like numbers, letters, and shapes.

Socialization and Playgroups


Emphasizing social skills, cooperation, and creative play.

Physical Education


Structured indoor and outdoor playtime to promote physical health and gross motor skills.

Enrichment Classes


Creative activities to foster artistic expression and fine motor skills. Introduction to music, rhythm, and basic movement exercises.

Excursions


Nature, cultural, educational, historical and community engagement trips.
